Who We Are

What is CyraX?

CyraX is an online learning platform I built as part of a web technologies module. The idea was to make something that actually looks and works like a real course website.

There are courses across three levels - foundation, undergraduate, and postgraduate. You can filter them, view lessons, and register for a course.

01 Structured pathways for foundation, undergraduate, and postgraduate learners
02 Flexible self-paced study designed for working schedules and personal commitments
03 Completion certificates suitable for professional and academic portfolios
