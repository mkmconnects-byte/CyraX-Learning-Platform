How it works

How to get started

1. Pick a level

Choose foundation, undergraduate, or postgraduate. There's something for most skill levels.

2. Go through the modules

Each course has modules. Just go through them one by one, no rush.

3. Do the tasks

There are assignments to help you practice what you learned.

4. Finish and get certified

Complete the assessments and you'll get a certificate at the end.

FAQ

Some common questions

Do I need experience before joining?

No, not really. There are beginner courses you can start with if you're new to this.

Can I go at my own pace?

Yes. There are no fixed schedules. You can start and stop whenever.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes, after you finish a course and pass the assessment you get a certificate.

How do I get help?

Go to the contact page and send a message. Someone should get back to you.
